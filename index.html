Welcome to iOrange 2

iOrange 2.0 is a free, simple and light-weight web template for your website and is valid HTML5 & CSS3 (W3C Compliant) document. The template is designed by Theme Bin. iOrange 2 is built on HTML5 Boiler Plate framework and is fully responsive with mobile-first attitude. The template weighs just under 60KB and is licensed under MIT. You are free to download, use, modify, share and make commercial usage. However, it is appreciated if you can keep the link to my site as credit in footer intact, but not necessary.

For more templates, themes, wallpapers and more, visit Theme Bin. Hope you find this template useful and best of luck!
